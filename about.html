About ATDC

Learn about Kenya's Agriculture Training Development Centers and our mission to modernize Kenyan agriculture through technology and innovation.

Our Mission

The Agricultural Training and Development Centers (ATDC) are dedicated to enhancing agricultural productivity in Kenya through practical training, technology transfer, and capacity building for farmers, extension officers, and agricultural stakeholders.

We aim to bridge the gap between agricultural research and implementation, ensuring that innovative farming technologies and practices reach those who need them most - Kenya's farming communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution in agricultural transformation in Kenya, fostering a sustainable, productive, and technologically advanced agricultural sector that contributes to national food security and economic growth.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our training programs and service delivery.

Innovation

We embrace and promote innovative approaches to agricultural challenges.

Collaboration

We work closely with partners to achieve greater impact in agricultural development.

Our History

The Agricultural Training and Development Centers (ATDC) were established in 2005 through a collaboration between the Government of Kenya and the Japanese International Cooperation Agency (JICA). The initial focus was on improving mechanization in Kenya's agricultural sector.

Over the years, our scope has expanded to include a wide range of agricultural practices, technologies, and value addition processes. Today, ATDC operates 10 centers across Kenya, each specializing in agricultural practices relevant to their ecological zones.

Through continuous improvement and adaptation, we have trained thousands of farmers, agricultural extension officers, and stakeholders, contributing significantly to the modernization of Kenya's agricultural sector.

What We Do

Practical Training Programs

We conduct hands-on training sessions for farmers, extension officers, and agricultural technicians on various aspects of farming, from land preparation to post-harvest management.

Curriculum Development

We develop and update training curricula that are relevant, practical, and aligned with the latest agricultural technologies and practices.

Agricultural Mechanization

We promote and provide training on appropriate mechanization technologies that enhance productivity and reduce labor costs for farmers.

Value Addition

We train farmers and SMEs on value addition techniques that help them increase the market value of their agricultural products and reduce post-harvest losses.

Climate-Smart Agriculture

We promote sustainable farming practices that are resilient to climate change and help farmers adapt to changing weather patterns.

Research and Development

We collaborate with research institutions to test and adapt new agricultural technologies to the Kenyan context, ensuring they are appropriate and beneficial for local farmers.
